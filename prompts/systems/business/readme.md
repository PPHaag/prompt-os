1/ Validate Before You Build

Prompt:
“Analyze my business idea: [BUSINESS IDEA] for [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Is there demand? Who are the top competitors? What’s the fastest version I can launch to test it today?”

2/ Craft an Irresistible Offer

Prompt:
“Design a high-value offer for my business that solves [SPECIFIC PROBLEM] for [AUDIENCE]. It should feel premium, be easy to deliver, and get people saying YES.”

3/ MVP in One Day

Prompt:
“Help me create a minimum viable version of [BUSINESS IDEA] I can launch in 24 hours using [MY SKILLS/TOOLS]. Focus on speed, simplicity, and customer results.”

4/ One-Page Website Copy

Prompt:
“Write all the content for a one-page website selling [MY PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Include a hook, benefits, pricing, FAQ, social proof (if needed), and a CTA that drives action.”

5/ Free Lead Machine

Prompt:
“Build a zero-cost lead generation plan for my [BUSINESS TYPE]. No ads. Just organic methods I can implement today to get my first 10 potential customers.”

6/ Sales Script That Sells

Prompt:
“Write me a short but powerful sales script to convert leads into paying clients for my [SERVICE/PRODUCT]. Handle common objections and push urgency.”

7/ Fake It ‘Til You Make It (Legally)

Prompt:
“How can I build instant social proof for my brand-new business — fast? Give me creative but ethical ways to get testimonials, trust signals, and momentum.”
